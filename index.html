Travel to...

if you..

  • like to move around
  • want to discover different cultures
  • don't necessarily like fish
  • just want to have a nice time
Then travelling is what's best for you. You can discover the mountains, lakes, food, get to know some people, then go to your next destination before the winter really hits. Learn about travelling to Norway here.

...or live in Norway?

if you...

  • would rather stay in one place
  • love the cold and snow
  • are crazy about fish or fishing
  • are close to nature
Then you can consider moving to Norway. Of course, travelling there first is a good option. Get to know the country a little bit, get a feel of what's living there like. But if you are certain about moving, this is a great website for you.
